About Me

Hello! I am a music-loving video maker who works full time for a travel website. My day to day work includes editing drone footage, communicating with voice actors, exploring editing ideas and producing shortform videos. I love film and everything that goes into it, but my favorite job I've ever had was working for Graywhale Entertainment. I worked there full time as an assistant manager for a little over 5 years. Working at a record store was a dream come true! I hope to always be involved in the local record store community. Beyond work, I have some hobbies. I love to play the piano, and I especially love just hanging out with my dog, Finny.
